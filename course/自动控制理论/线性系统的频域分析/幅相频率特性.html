幅相频率特性（Nyquist图）

比例环节

        G(s)=K                    G(jω)=K~~~~~~~~G(s) = K~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~G(j\omega) = K        G(s)=K                    G(jω)=K

        ∣G∣=K                      ∠G=0∘~~~~~~~~|G| = K~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~\angle G = 0^\circ        ∣G∣=K                      ∠G=0∘

  

微分环节

        G(s)=s                    G(jω)=jω~~~~~~~~G(s) = s~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~G(j\omega) = j\omega        G(s)=s                    G(jω)=jω

        ∣G∣=ω                      ∠G=90∘~~~~~~~~|G| = \omega~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~\angle G = 90^\circ        ∣G∣=ω                      ∠G=90∘

  

积分环节

        G(s)=1s                    G(jω)=1jω~~~~~~~~G(s) = \frac{1}{s}~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~G(j\omega) = \frac{1}{j\omega}        G(s)=s1​                    G(jω)=jω1​

        ∣G∣=1ω                      ∠G=−90∘~~~~~~~~|G| = \frac{1}{\omega}~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~\angle G = -90^\circ        ∣G∣=ω1​                      ∠G=−90∘

  

惯性环节

        G(s)=1Ts+1              G(jω)=1jωT+1~~~~~~~~G(s) = \frac{1}{Ts+1}~~~~~~~~~~~~~~G(j\omega) = \frac{1}{j\omega T+1}        G(s)=Ts+11​              G(jω)=jωT+11​

        ∣G∣=11+ω2T2            ∠G=−arctan ωT~~~~~~~~|G| = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1+\omega^2T^2}}~~~~~~~~~~~~\angle G = -arctan~\omega T        ∣G∣=1+ω2T2​1​            ∠G=−arctan ωT

  

一阶微分环节

        G(s)=Ts+1           G(jω)=jωT+1~~~~~~~~G(s) = Ts+1~~~~~~~~~~~G(j\omega) = j\omega T+1        G(s)=Ts+1           G(jω)=jωT+1

        ∣G∣=1+ω2T2      ∠G=arctan ωT~~~~~~~~|G| = \sqrt{1+\omega^2T^2}~~~~~~\angle G = arctan~\omega T        ∣G∣=1+ω2T2​      ∠G=arctan ωT
